Villa Donna

Villa Donna is a fenced estate offering exclusivity to its guests with 500 sqm of garden, a 50 sqm private heated pool, secure parking and unimpeded view to the Gulf of Kyparissia. The villa consists of two private residences, with the one in the upper floor not being offered at the moment. At the pool level, the private residence of 100 sqm offers two cosy bedrooms, a spacious living room with a comfortable sofabed, large openings, a covered porch, a fully equipped kitchen, an ethanol fireplace, Smart TV in all rooms, a bathroom with a bathtub and a WC with a shower, all elegantly furnished.

This home-like charming villa, located only 5 min away from the local market, the old town, the medieval castle, and beautiful sandy beaches, can comfortably accommodate 4 people, with a max capacity of 6.

Kyparissia is 245km northwest of Athens (2h 50 min), 46km northwest of Kalamata Airport (45 min), 130km southeast of Araxos Airport (1h 30 min) and 150km southeast of Patras Harbor. It is a bay of the Ionian Sea and one of the main breeding grounds of the endangered loggerhead sea turtle (kareta-kareta).
