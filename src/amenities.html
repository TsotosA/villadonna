Villa Donna

Villa Donna is a fenced estate with an exclusive residence of 100 sq.m., a 50 sq.m. private heated pool and 500 sq.m. of garden. It has two bedrooms, a living room with a comfortable sofabed, large openings, a fully equipped kitchen, an ethanol fireplace, Smart TV in all rooms, a bathroom with a bathtub and a WC with a shower, all elegantly furnished. This home-like charming Villa, ideal for young retirees, can comfortably accommodate 4 people, with a max capacity of 6. Kyparissia is 245 km northwest of Athens (2h 50 min), 46 km northwest of Kalamata Airport (45 min), 130 km southeast of Araxos Airport (1h 30 min) and 150km southeast of Patras Harbor. It is a bay of the Ionian Sea and one of the main breeding grounds of the endangered loggerhead sea turtle (kareta-kareta).
