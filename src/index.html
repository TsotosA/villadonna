In today’s modern lifestyle, holidays are meant a lot. Embracing the principles of Greek hospitality, Villa Donna at Kyparissia Gulf in Messinia Peloponnese offers a private heaven of rest, full of stunning sea views, unique sunsets and amenities. It is an ideal destination for the ones who love to relax in privacy and serenity, away from mass tourism.

Kyparissia, a charming town in Messinia Greece, offers a variety of attractions and activities for visitors. Explore the traditional architecture and stunning views at Kyparissia’s Castle. Further south, enjoy scenic drives along Navarino Bay's coastline, visits to traditional fishing villages and swim in numerous sandy beaches like the renowned Voidokilia Beach. Experience olive oil tastings and indulge in thrilling water sports along the Ionian Sea. Delve into history at the impressive Ancient Messene and visit the birthplace of the Olympic Games in Ancient Olympia. Day trips to the vibrant seaside city of Kalamata, scenic Pylos, Methoni, and picturesque Finikounta promise cultural and culinary experiences, while birdwatching at Gialova Lagoon and golfing at Costa Navarino Golf Resort offer diverse experiences for every traveler's preferences.

 

Kyparissia is easy to access by car. It is 245km of highway drive northwest of Athens (2h 50 min), or 46km northwest of Kalamata Airport (45 min), 130km southeast of Araxos Airport (1h 30 min) and 150km southeast of Patras Harbor.
